AVANT

The name ‘avant’ originates from the French term ‘avant-garde’ which stands for being artistically and culturally ahead. It is characterized by aesthetic innovation and is a hallmark of modernism.

WHO WE ARE

'Avant Experiential' is a versatile art-décor company specializing in art installations, sets and decor. 

Our forte lies in transforming spaces with 3-dimensional works of art. We deliver innovative and experiential art-décor solutions from design to manufacturing and execution. With our expertise in artistic installations, spatial decor, retail design, events and weddings, we craft stellar experiences for every space and occasion. 

WHAT WE DO

We redefine the perception of spaces with stunning sensory experiences.

We offer a wide range of art-décor solutions that include permanent & semi-permanent art installations, large scale mixed-media installations, concept installation-art, customized & concept-based decor, thematic sets, spatial décor, retail pop-ups, window displays and VM. With our expertise in concept, design and execution, we provide turnkey solutions for all space design needs.

Our designs are modern, contemporary and fresh. Our work is characterized by A+ grade select material and top-of-the-line craftsmanship. We use the best quality material fit-outs and latest technology to create the most innovative and experiential art-décor solutions for your brand.

SERVICES
Mall Décor & Installations


Malls have become vibrant shopping spaces that enhance community experiences. Our team of conceptualizers and visualizers specialize in experiential mall décor and artistic installations for all festivals, celebrations and events. Our artistic installations and concept-based décor are truly a hallmark of aesthetic innovation. 

Urban Space Décor & Installations


Premium urban spaces like airports, corporate Parks, 5-star hotels, theme parks and the like, have now become experiential spaces that offer much more. We design and execute enthralling festive décor and installations, transforming these urban spaces into unique urban experiences.


Public Space Installations


Be it a recreational park, a walking promenade, a government office, or a roadway attraction point, we create grandiose mixed media art installations for all types of public spaces. Public space installations give an aesthetic character to your space, are inspiring and carry a message for the people.


Art Installations


Make your space come alive with our permanent art installations, concept installation art, 3-dimensional visual art and large-scale installations. Every art installation is tailor-made fulfilling your decor objectives. We understand your brief in detail and create that perfect masterpiece for your space. 

Spatial Decor


We offer complete spatial design and décor solutions for your venue, giving your interiors a fresh and new look that evokes awe. We work closely with architects to chart out the best art & décor solutions for your space, and partner with your technical team at every step to create that stunning ambience.


Brand Installations


A brand installation is a unique artistic representation of your brand’s identity. We create installations that perfectly fit your brand objectives for all your experiential marketing campaigns and events. Our installations are innovative, aesthetic and modern, enhancing the visual imagery of your brand. 


Retail


Our retail design studio specializes in design and execution of impactful retail experiences that propel your brand further to a larger audience. We create unique and attractive pop-ups, window displays and VM installations that provide an environment for the customer to experience and interact with your brand.  

Events


Move away from the standard event, raise the bar and create an immersive brand experience with our extravagant sets, decor and installations. We understand that every event is an opportunity to celebrate your brand and that’s why we focus on creating a stunning sensory experience for your brand.


Weddings


Our visionary wedding team brings their years of experience and expertise to make your dream wedding unravel beautifully in front of your eyes. We specialize in design and execution of customized decor, grand thematic sets, artistic installations, floral décor, fabrics, furniture and lighting.
